Space Tracer

A space action game inspired by Space Invaders and Galaga. Built for touchscreen mobile devices and available for free!

Google Play Games Integration

Sign in with a Google Play Games account and gain access to unlockable achievements and a high score leaderboard!

Addictive Endless Gameplay

As long as your ship's health does not reach zero the waves of enemies will continue to loop. Survive as long as possible to rack up a big high score, unlock achievements and to make the galaxy a safer place!

Simple & Fun Touchscreen Control

Using the touchscreen tap and drag on the ship to dodge lasers, ships, bombs and to collect powerups to survive!

Dominate the High Scores

With Google Play Games you can aim for the best daily, weekly and all time High Scores to compare and compete with friends and players all over the world.
